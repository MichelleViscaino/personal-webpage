Hi, I'm Michelle from Ecuador

Did you know? Due to Earth's shape, Ecuador's Mount Chimborazo is closer to space than even Mount Everest!

I'm passionate about creating cutting-edge technology for medical applications. My journey started with receiving a B.S. degree in electronic engineering from Escuela Politécnica Nacional, Quito, Ecuador, in 2016. After that, I continued with a PhD in electronic engineering at Universidad Técnica Federico Santa María, Valparaíso, Chile, in 2023.

During this fantastic time, I met my true love: artificial intelligence applied to medical problems.

Research

My research focuses on the intersection of artificial intelligence and healthcare, particularly in developing models that improve medical diagnostics. I am especially interested in the application of machine learning to medical imaging and signal processing to assist healthcare professionals in making more accurate and timely decisions.

Teaching

I have had the privilege of teaching several courses related to electronic engineering, artificial intelligence, and data science. Sharing knowledge and mentoring students is a passion of mine, and I find great joy in seeing students develop their skills and become independent researchers.
